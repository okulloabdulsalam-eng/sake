Donate to Kisilaahe

Support the Kisilaahe community by donating essential food items. Your contributions help provide basic necessities for those in need.

Required Items:

  • Rice (all forms)
  • Sugar
  • Posho
  • Beans

Contact for Donations:

0787749111 WhatsApp: 0787749111

Call or WhatsApp to arrange your donation

You can also make a monetary donation through our payment page.

Kizumu Tahfiz Charity Visit

Join us in supporting the Kizumu Tahfiz community through our charity visit. Your contributions help provide essential resources for Islamic education.

Required Items:

  • 900 kgs of Posho
  • 300 kgs of Rice
  • Yasarunas
  • Qurans
  • Quran Seats

To contribute or volunteer, please contact us or make a donation through the payment page.
