Activities

Every Saturday

Community Sports Day

Join us for friendly football matches, cricket, and other sports activities. Open to all ages and skill levels. Refreshments provided.

Weekly

Community Iftar

Breaking fast together during Ramadan. Enjoy delicious meals, spiritual reflection, and community bonding. All are welcome.

Monthly

Volunteer Work

Help those in need through various community service projects. Food drives, visiting the elderly, and supporting local charities.

Bi-weekly

Arts & Crafts Workshop

Creative workshops for children and adults. Learn Islamic calligraphy, pottery, painting, and other artistic skills.

Seasonal

Nature Walks & Outings

Explore the beautiful UK countryside with family-friendly hikes and nature activities. Perfect for all fitness levels.

Weekly

Youth Gaming & Social

Safe space for youth to enjoy gaming, board games, and social activities in an Islamic environment.
