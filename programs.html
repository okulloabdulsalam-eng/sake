Our Programs

Weekly

Quran Study Circle

Join us every Friday evening for in-depth study of the Holy Quran. Learn tafsir, tajweed, and memorization techniques in a supportive environment.

Monthly

Islamic Studies Course

Comprehensive courses covering Aqeedah, Fiqh, Seerah, and Islamic History. Suitable for beginners and advanced students.

Saturday & Sunday

Children's Islamic Education

Fun and engaging Islamic education for children aged 5-16. Includes Quran memorization, Arabic language, and Islamic manners.

Tuesday & Thursday

Arabic Language Classes

Learn Arabic from beginner to advanced levels. Focus on reading, writing, speaking, and understanding Classical Arabic.

Weekly

Sisters Circle

A safe space for sisters to learn, share, and grow together. Topics include Islamic jurisprudence, family matters, and personal development.

Flexible

Online Learning Platform

Access courses, lectures, and resources anytime, anywhere. Perfect for those with busy schedules.
