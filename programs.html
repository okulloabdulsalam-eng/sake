Schedule Updates

Schedule Updates: The schedule may be adjusted during Ramadan, holidays, or special events. Please check our Events page for updates.

Venue

Venue: All sessions are held at the KIUMA Masjid/Office unless otherwise announced.

Attendance

Attendance: All students are welcome to attend. No prior registration required (except for a few classes), but regular attendance is encouraged.

Materials

Materials: Some sessions may require textbooks or materials. Contact the office for more information.

Prayer Times

Prayer Times: Sessions are scheduled around prayer times. Please arrive on time and be ready for prayer.

Weekly

Quran Study Circle

Join us every Monday to thursday evening for in-depth study of the Holy Quran. Learn tafsir, tajweed, and memorization techniques in a supportive environment.

Monthly

Islamic Studies Course

Comprehensive courses covering Aqeedah, Fiqh, Seerah, and Islamic History. Suitable for beginners and advanced students.

Teusday

Hadith memorisation circle

Learn the ways of the prophet PBUH(worship and daily living), memorise them and get there application.

Saturday & Sunday

Arabic Language Classes

Learn Arabic from beginner to advanced levels. Focus on reading, writing, speaking, and understanding Classical Arabic.

Weekly

Sisters Circle

A safe space for sisters to learn, share, and grow together. Topics include Islamic jurisprudence, family matters, and personal development.

Flexible

Online Learning Platform

Access courses, lectures, and resources anytime, anywhere. Perfect for those with busy schedules.
