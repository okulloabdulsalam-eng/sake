  • Tawheed (Oneness of Allah) - Common misunderstandings
  • Bid'ah (Innovation) - What is and isn't Bid'ah
  • Intercession (Shafa'ah) - Correct understanding

  • Jihad - True meaning and application

Few Important Hadiths of the Prophet (PBUH)

Essential sayings and teachings of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) that every Muslim should know.

Hadith 1:

"Actions are but by intention, and every man shall have but that which he intended..."

- Sahih al-Bukhari

Hadith 2:

"None of you truly believes until he loves for his brother what he loves for himself."

- Sahih al-Bukhari

Hadith 3:

"The best of people are those who are most beneficial to people."

- Al-Mu'jam al-Awsat

Hadith 4:

"Whoever believes in Allah and the Last Day should speak good or remain silent."

- Sahih al-Bukhari

Did You Know

Interesting and beneficial Islamic facts and knowledge.

Did you know?

The word "Salah" (prayer) is mentioned 99 times in the Quran, emphasizing its importance in a Muslim's life.

Did you know?

The first revelation to Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) was "Iqra" (Read), highlighting the importance of knowledge in Islam.

Did you know?

Smiling at your brother is considered a form of charity (Sadaqah) in Islam.

Did you know?

The Prophet (PBUH) said that seeking knowledge is obligatory upon every Muslim, male and female.
