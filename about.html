About Us

Our Mission

KIUMA (Kampala International University Muslim Students Association) is dedicated to fostering a strong, vibrant Islamic community at Kampala International University. We aim to provide spiritual guidance, educational resources, and social support to Muslim students while promoting understanding and harmony in our diverse university community.

Our Vision

To be the leading platform for Islamic education, community engagement, and spiritual growth at Kampala International University, creating a welcoming space where every Muslim student can thrive and contribute positively to the university and society.

Our History

Founded with a vision to unite Muslim students at Kampala International University, KIUMA has been serving the Muslim student community for many years. We started as a small initiative and have grown into a comprehensive platform offering various programs and services to support students in their faith and education.

Our Community

We serve Muslim students at Kampala International University, providing educational programs, religious services, community events, and support networks. Our community includes students from diverse backgrounds and various programs of study.

Get Involved

Join us in building a stronger Islamic community. Whether you want to participate in programs, volunteer, or support our initiatives, there's a place for everyone at KIUMA.

Our Values

Faith (Iman)

We are committed to strengthening faith through authentic Islamic teachings, regular worship, and spiritual growth. Our programs are designed to deepen understanding and connection with Allah.

Compassion (Rahma)

Compassion is at the heart of everything we do. We extend kindness, empathy, and support to all members of our community, following the example of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

Knowledge (Ilm)

We believe in the pursuit of knowledge as a lifelong journey. Education in both Islamic and worldly matters is essential for personal growth and community development.

Unity (Ittihad)

We promote unity within the Muslim community and with our neighbors. Despite our diverse backgrounds, we stand together as one Ummah, supporting and uplifting each other.

Justice (Adl)

Justice is a fundamental Islamic principle. We advocate for fairness, equality, and standing up against injustice in all its forms, within our community and beyond.

Integrity (Amanah)

We conduct all our affairs with honesty, transparency, and trustworthiness. Integrity guides our decisions, financial practices, and interactions with the community.

Service (Khidmah)

Service to others is service to Allah. We are dedicated to helping those in need, supporting the vulnerable, and contributing positively to society.

Respect (Ihtiram)

We treat everyone with dignity and respect, regardless of age, gender, ethnicity, or background. We value diverse perspectives and create inclusive spaces for all.
