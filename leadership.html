Leadership

Imam Ahmed Hassan

Director & Lead Imam

With over 20 years of experience in Islamic education and community leadership, Imam Ahmed guides our community with wisdom and compassion. He holds a degree in Islamic Studies from Al-Azhar University.

Dr. Fatima Ali

Educational Director

Dr. Fatima oversees all educational programs and curriculum development. She has a PhD in Islamic Education and has been instrumental in developing our children's and adult learning programs.

Mohammed Khan

Community Affairs Coordinator

Mohammed manages community outreach, events, and partnerships. He works to build bridges with other communities and organizations while serving the needs of our members.

Aisha Rahman

Sisters Programs Coordinator

Aisha leads all programs and activities for sisters. She creates safe spaces for women to learn, grow, and connect while maintaining Islamic principles and values.

Yusuf Ibrahim

Youth Programs Director

Yusuf develops and manages programs for youth and young adults. He creates engaging activities that help young Muslims stay connected to their faith and community.

Board of Trustees

Our Board of Trustees consists of dedicated community members who provide oversight, strategic direction, and ensure transparency in all our operations. They volunteer their time and expertise to serve the community.

  • Dr. Sarah Ahmed - Vice Chairman
