Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate Zakat?

Zakat is 2.5% of your total wealth that has been held for one lunar year, above the nisab threshold. This includes cash, gold, silver, and trade goods.

What are the prayer times?

Prayer times vary by location and change daily. Check the home page for current prayer times, or use our mobile app for accurate times based on your location.

How can I join the community?

You can register online through our "Join Us" page, attend our events, or visit our community center. All are welcome!
