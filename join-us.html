Help & FAQ

How do I create an account?

Click "Create Account" on the login screen, enter your name, email, and password. You'll be automatically signed in after registration.

How do I enroll in programs?

Go to "Join Programs" from the menu or home page. Fill in your details and select the courses you want to join. An email will be sent to confirm your enrollment.

How do I check prayer times?

Prayer times are displayed on the home page. The current prayer is highlighted. Times are based on the Umm al-Qura calculation method.

How do I enable notifications?

Go to Settings in your account and enable "Push Notifications". You'll receive updates about events, programs, and important announcements.

How do I make payments?

Use the "Pay" section to contribute to KIUMA activities. You can pay for dues, donations, or program fees through Mobile Money or other supported methods.

Still have questions? Contact the developer via WhatsApp for assistance.
